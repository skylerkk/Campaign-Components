About Baby Yoda

Baby Yoda comes from unkown origins. Although appearing small and young Baby Yoda is estimated to be 50 years old. He is force sensitive giving him the ability to do an assortment of force powers including: telekinetics, healing, and force barriers. He is a kind and trustworthy person which alone would make him a great canidate for constable, but he also has force powers which would bring any wrong doers to justice.
